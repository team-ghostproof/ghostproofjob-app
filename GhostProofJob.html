🚀 Quick Start Guide
✕
Welcome to GhostProofJob 👻 Here's where everything lives — three steps: Build · Optimize · Apply.
1️⃣ Build — 📄 Resume tab
Upload your resume (or import from LinkedIn) and we auto-fill your profile. Edit your title, experience, education, skills, and summary anytime. Tap ✨ Improve with Jett — your résumé co-pilot — to sharpen your summary and each role’s key duties (in an editable box you control).
2️⃣ Optimize — your match scores
Every job shows a match % and a 👻 ghost risk %. Tap ✨ Optimize on any job to tailor a cover letter or resume to it. We never invent experience — we sharpen what's real.
3️⃣ Apply — 🎴 Swipe & 🔍 Browse
Swipe: swipe right to apply, left to skip. Browse: the same full job cards as Swipe (Match to Job, Cover Letter, full company profile, View Full Posting) — plus a 🚫 Not interested button. Jobs you act on in one place won't reappear in the other.
📂 Where your stuff lives
🔖 Saved Jobs & Companies → Account page, under My Activity (right here).
📝 Cover letters & tailored resumes → created when you tap ✨ Optimize on a job; download or copy them right there. Your generated resume lives on the 📄 Resume tab under Download / Preview.
📊 Applied / Skipped / Responses → tracked automatically; see your counts on the Account page.
🤖 Preview what an ATS sees → on the Resume tab, under the Download button — shows exactly how applicant-tracking systems read your resume.
📖 Button & term glossary
Match % — How well a job fits your title, skills, and work history. Higher = better fit for you.
👻 Ghost Risk % — How likely a posting is fake, stale, or an evergreen \u201calways hiring\u201d trap. Lower is safer.
Match to Job — Tailors your resume\u2019s wording to a specific job\u2019s keywords so it passes that employer\u2019s ATS scanner. Use it per-job, right before you apply.
✨ Optimize — Generates a cover letter tuned to that exact role, ready to copy or download.
✨ Improve with Jett (Resume tab) — Jett is your AI résumé co-pilot. It rewrites your Professional Summary and each role’s Key Duties from your real experience, in an editable box you can tweak before keeping. Tries internal tools first; AI assist is ~2×/month per section so it stays free for everyone.
Green Flag — A trust signal, like a verified, posted salary. Good sign the listing is real.
🚫 Not interested (Browse) — Skips a job and frees the slot for fresh listings.
⚡ Hyper-Drive / Core Search / Base Camp — Your access tiers as your hunt progresses (see your account for current status). Hyper-Drive is unlimited; the others give generous daily limits — always free.
