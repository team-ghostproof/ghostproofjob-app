## Privacy Policy

**The short version:** We exist to get you hired, not to monetize you. We never sell your data. We show no ads. Our only revenue is the optional $12 lifetime purchase and tips.

### 1. What We Collect
Contact details, employment history, education, your uploaded resume, and company ratings you submit. **Location is opt-in only.**

### 2. How We Use It
To auto-fill applications you approve, compute your job-match scores, and surface nearby roles. Personal data is securely processed via **Firebase / Firestore** to facilitate cross-device syncing tied to your authenticated account. Companies parsed from your resume are added to our public database **anonymously** (no personal identifiers attached).

### 3. What We Never Do
- No selling or renting of your data.
- No advertising profiles.
- No tracking you across other websites.

### 4. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)
Export everything, correct anything, or delete your account and all associated data from **Settings** at any time. Deletion is permanent.

### 5. Data Retention
Active accounts are kept while in use. Accounts inactive for **24+ months** are anonymized.

### 6. Data Security & Sync
Your data is bound to your authenticated user identity. When you sign out, your working data is cleared from the device; the authoritative copy lives in your cloud account and is restored on your next sign-in on any device. While we apply reasonable safeguards, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure, and — consistent with Section 6 of the Terms above — the Developer is not liable for data loss, sync anomalies, or corruption.
